About Us

A wholly Australian owned family run company, established by Roy Bare in 1944. BARE-Co has grown to be the largest manufacturer / wholesale distributor of pattern agricultural tractor parts in the southern hemisphere. The company operates a modern C.N.C equipped manufacturing plant within its Sydney headquarters. Company policy dictates, the eight Australian, two New Zealand and two US operation carry every line shown on the BARE-Co website.
